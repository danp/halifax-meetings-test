7.1.1 Case 22865: Rezoning Application, PID 41489048, Sackville Drive, Middle Sackville [PDF]
Motion:
That North West Community Council give First Reading to consider approval of the proposed amendment to the Land Use By-law for Sackville, as set out in Attachment A of the staff recommendation report dated August 13, 2020, to rezone lands known as PID 41489048 on Sackville Drive, Middle Sackville from the R-6 Zone to the R-4 Zone, and schedule a public hearing.

7.1.2 Case 22450: Amendments to the Existing Development Agreement for Bedford West, Sub Area 9, Bedford and Halifax [PDF]
Motion:
That North West Community Council give notice of motion to consider the proposed amending agreement, as set out in Attachment A of the staff recommendation report dated July 22, 2020, to allow the reallocation of commercial density to residential density in Bedford West, Sub Area 9 and schedule a public hearing to be held concurrently with the Halifax and West Community Council.


7.2 MEMBERS OF NORTH WEST COMMUNITY COUNCIL

7.2.1 Case 19117: Development Agreement for 525-unit Mobile Home Park on the Old Truro Road, Enfield [PDF]
Motion:
That North West Community Council:
1. Amend the June 22, 2020 motion to defer giving notice of motion to Case 19117 to remove the following line: “, for approximately six (6) months until such time that in-person public hearings can safely resume.”
2. Give notice of motion to consider the proposed development agreement, as set out in Attachment A of the staff report dated May 4, 2020, to permit a 525-unit mobile home park off Old Truro Road, Enfield and schedule a public hearing in November 2020.
