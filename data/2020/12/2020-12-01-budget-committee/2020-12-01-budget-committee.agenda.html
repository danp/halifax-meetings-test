Would you like to speak during Public Participation?
If you’d like to speak during Public Participation, contact the Municipal Clerk's Office no later than 4:30 p.m. on Monday, November 30, to be added to the speakers' list to speak by telephone.  This meeting will occur in a virtual setting and attending in person is not possible. To register as a speaker:
-    Send an email to clerks@halifax.ca
-    Phone 902.490.4210 (Mon-Fri, 8:30 am-4:30 pm)
-    Fax 902.490.4208

In your message, state that you wish to speak during Public Participation at the December 1 Budget Committee meeting, and give your name, community of residence, email address, and the phone number that you’ll use to participate.

Motion:
That Budget Committee recommend that Halifax Regional Council:

2. Approve the development of a 2021-2025 Strategic Priorities Plan and 2021-2025 Administrative Priority Plan and associated annual reporting process;
3. Approve the discontinuation of the Budget Allocator tool in favour of Online Business Planning and Budget Survey;
4. Approve the 2021/22 Committee of the Whole Business Planning and Budget Meeting Schedule (Attachment B of the staff report dated November 18, 2020.)
