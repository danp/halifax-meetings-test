12. MOTIONS

12.1 Deputy Mayor Johns

(i) That Council direct the Mayor write to the Minister of Transportation and Infrastructure Renewal, the Honourable Bill Estabrooks, to request that the Provincial Government investigate funding opportunities that would generate income to offset the cost of substantial transportation initiatives in HRM; particularly, transit operations of the Municipality.

(ii) That Council direct the Mayor write to the Minister responsible for Finance with the Nova Scotia Provincial Government, the Honourable Graham Steele, and request that the Halifax Dartmouth Bridge Commission apply to the Nova Scotia Utility and Review Board, requesting a fare increase of twenty-five cents; and that fifteen cents of this increase be provided to the Halifax Regional Municipality to offset the cost of substantial transportation initiatives, particularly transit and existing ferry operations.
