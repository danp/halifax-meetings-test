10. MOTIONS

10.1 Councillor Hum
"To amend vote requirements for the trial installation of traffic calming measures under the Neighbourhood Short Cutting Policy. Presently, a simple majority of the valid returned ballots of the households and businesses on the problem street(s) are required for the plan to go to staff for consideration of a test. For permanent installation, only a simple majority support of the proposed measures is required. I will be requesting the vote for the trial measures be amended to be consistent with the requirement for the permanent installation which is substantially less rigorous."
