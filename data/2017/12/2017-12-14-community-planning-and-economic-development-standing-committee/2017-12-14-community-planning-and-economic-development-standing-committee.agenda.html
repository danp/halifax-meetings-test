12.2 MEMBERS OF STANDING COMMITTEE

12.2.1 Councillor Austin – Potential Changes to By-Law N-200 respecting Noise [PDF]

Motion:
That the Community Planning & Economic Development Standing Committee request a staff report outlining options for potential changes to By-Law N-200 Respecting Noise to better align prohibited times for construction related noise with reasonable standards when
adjacent to residential areas.

12.2.2 Councillor Smith – Long term streetscaping program for the Regional Centre [PDF]

Motion:
That the Community Planning and Economic Development Standing Committee:

1. Request a staff report regarding options and opportunities for a long term streetscaping program for the Regional Centre that updates the rationale and priorities for projects, and establishes stable long term funding; and
2. That staff review the letter received by the urban core Business Improvement Districts.

12.3 HERITAGE ADVISORY COMMITTEE

12.3.1 Future Heritage Conservation District Timeframe Review [PDF]
