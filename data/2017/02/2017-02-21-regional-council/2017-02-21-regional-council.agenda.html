14. REPORTS
 14.1 CHIEF ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER
14.1.1 Federal Contribution Agreements – Public Transit Infrastructure Fund [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council authorize the Mayor and Municipal Clerk to sign the fifteen Contribution Agreements with the Minister of Municipal Affairs, to receive funding for fifteen public transit projects approved under the Public Transit Infrastructure Fund (PTIF) as describe in staff report dated January 5, 2017.

14.1.2 Proposed Administrative Order 2016-004-ADM, Respecting Variance Notification Distance [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council adopt Administrative Order 2016-004-ADM, Respecting Variance Notification Distance as set out in Attachment A of the staff report dated January 23, 2017.

14.1.3 Proposed Administrative Order 2016-008-ADM, Respecting the Reduction of Taxes Payable for Certain Residential Properties – Stormwater Right of Way (ROW) Reduction Options [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council adopt Administrative Order 2016-008-ADM, the Tax Reduction Administrative Order, as set out in Attachment 2 of the staff report dated January 24, 2017 to provide certain residential properties a one- time tax reduction in April 2017, as set out in the discussion section of the staff report dated January 24, 2017.

14.2 ENVIRONMENT AND SUSTAINABILITY STANDING COMMITTEE
14.2.1 Cogswell Redevelopment Area District Energy System [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council:
 1. Endorse pursuing the requirement that new development within the Cogswell redevelopment area connect to a district energy system if one exists;
 2. Seek amendments to the HRM Charter and other amendments as required to provide the general authority to implement District Energy Systems, including the Cogswell Redevelopment Area District Energy System; and
 3. Direct staff to develop the options and mechanisms to effectively implement the requirement for mandatory connection.

14.2.2 National Zero Waste Council Management Board Alternate [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council appoint the Manager of Solid Waste Resources as an alternate representative to the National Zero Waste Council Management Board, for a term to October 31, 2017.

14.3 HERITAGE ADVISORY COMMITTEE
14.3.1 Case H00430 – Request to Register 59 Hawthorne Street, Dartmouth as a Municipal Heritage Property [PDF]

 That Halifax Regional Council set a date for a heritage hearing to consider the heritage registration of 59 Hawthorne Street, Dartmouth as shown on Map 1 of the December 6, 2016 staff report as a Municipal Heritage Property.

14.3.2 Case H00439 – Request to Register the Christ Church Cemetery, (PID 00129973), Victoria Road, Dartmouth as a Municipal Heritage Property [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council set a date for a heritage hearing to consider the heritage registration of the Christ Church Cemetery, (PID 00129973), Victoria Road, Dartmouth as shown on Map 1 of the November 21, 2016 staff report as a Municipal Heritage Property.

14.3.3 Case H00440 – Request to Register 68 Hawthorne Street, Dartmouth as a Municipal Heritage Property [PDF]
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council set a date for a heritage hearing to consider the heritage registration of 68 Hawthorne Street, Dartmouth as shown on Map 1 of the December 21, 2016 staff report as a Municipal Heritage Property.

15. MOTIONS
 15.1 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 8, 2017

15.2 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 8, 2017
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council request a staff report outlining the cost of installing sidewalk on the Herring Cove Road from Lynnett Road to 564 Herring Cove Road including potential sources of funding from other municipal funds and with a view to having this installation take place in 2017/18.
This item has been deleted from the agenda.

15.3 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 8, 2017
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council ratify the decision of Committee of the Whole Council to defeat the motion to request a staff report for the 2018/19 budget regarding costs, savings and other implications, subject to a public consultation and a poll of the residents, for removing sidewalk snow removal from the areas of District 7, 8, 9 where it was introduced in 2013.
This item has been deleted from the agenda.

15.4 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 1, 2017
Motion:
 That Halifax Regional Council direct staff to provide a report analyzing the implications of an increase in the Parks and Recreation operating & capital budgets to 80% of municipal cost benchmarks by fiscal 2021/2022, increasing each of four years starting in fiscal 18/19, with funds to improve facility and field conditions, expand programming and grant funding for community events, arts, culture and heritage programming.

15.5 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 15, 2017

This item has been deleted from the agenda.

15.6 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 15, 2017
Motion:

This item has been deleted from the agenda.

15.7 Ratification of Budget Committee of the Whole Motion from February 15, 2017
Motion:

This item has been deleted from the agenda.
