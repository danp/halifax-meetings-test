6.1 Tim Boudreau - Unauthorized Removal of Vegetation from Point Pleasant Park [PDF]
Motion:
 That the Point Pleasant Park Advisory Committee recommend that Halifax and West Community Council request a staff report to consider the creation and implementation of a public awareness campaign regarding the unauthorized removal of vegetation from Point Pleasant Park including the installation of “Do Not Pick the Plants” signs as well as print and online advertising. It is further recommended that the report consider how the by-laws governing this matter can be enforced.
