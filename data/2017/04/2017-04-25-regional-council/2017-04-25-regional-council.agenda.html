14.3 TRANSPORTATION STANDING COMMITTEE
14.3.1 Traffic Control Persons for Special Events [PDF]
Motion:
That Halifax Regional Council:
1. Request that the Government of Nova Scotia amend the Nova Scotia Motor Vehicle Act to permit Traffic Control Persons to direct the movement of traffic for special events.
2. Request that the Government of Nova Scotia modify the Province’s training and accreditation processes to reflect special events-related roles for Traffic Control Persons and Temporary Workplace Signers.
3. Request that the Government of Nova Scotia revise the Province’s TC Manual to include standards, principles and guidelines applicable to special events-related traffic control scenarios.
