13.1 STAFF

13.1.1 Case 20945: Development Agreement Discharge - 874 Sackville Drive, Sackville [PDF]

Motion:
That North West Community Council:

1. Approve, by resolution, the proposed Discharge Agreement, which shall be substantially of the same form as set out in Attachment A of the staff report dated May 19, 2017; and

2.Require the Discharge Agreement be signed by the property owner within 120 days, or any extension thereof granted by Council on request of the property owner, from the date of final approval by Council and any other bodies as necessary, including applicable appeal periods, whichever is later; otherwise this approval will be void and obligations arising hereunder shall be at an end.

13.1.2 Case 20211: Amendments to the Municipal Planning Strategy and Land Use By-law for Bedford [PDF]

Motion:
That North West Community Council recommend that Regional Council:

1. Give First Reading to consider the proposed amendments to the Municipal Planning Strategy (MPS) and Land Use By-law (LUB) for Bedford, as set out in Attachments A and B of the staff report dated May 26, 2017, to permit service stations in portions of the Bedford Commons commercial area within the Light Industrial (ILI) Zone, Damascus Road, Bedford and schedule a public hearing; and

2. Approve the proposed amendments to the MPS and the LUB for Bedford, as set out in Attachments A and B of the staff report dated May 26, 2017.

13.1.3 Case 20216:  Non-Substantive Amendment to Royale Hemlocks Stage II Development Agreements, Starboard Drive and Cutter Drive, Halifax [PDF]

Motion:

That North West Community Council:

1. Approve, by resolution, the proposed Stage II amending agreements which shall be substantially of the same form as contained in Attachment B and Attachment C of the staff report dated July 7, 2017; and

2. Require the amending agreements be signed by the affected property owners within 200 days, or any extension thereof granted by Council on request of the applicant, from the date of final approval by Council and any other bodies as necessary, including applicable appeal periods, whichever is later; otherwise this approval will be void and obligations arising hereunder shall be at an end.

13.1.4 North West Community Council Planning Advisory Committees [PDF]

Motion:
That North West Community Council:
1. Dissolve the St. Margaret’s Bay Coastal Planning Advisory Committee and repeal the associated
Terms of Reference; and
2. Adopt the amendments to the Terms of Reference for the North West Planning Advisory Committee,
as set out in Attachment 3 of the staff report dated June 23, 2017, to include all of Districts 13 and 16.
