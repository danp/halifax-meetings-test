The Committee may rise and go into a private In Camera session, in accordance with Section 19 of the Halifax Regional Municipality Charter, for the purpose of dealing with the following:

Citizen appointments to boards and committees in keeping with the Public Appointment Policy adopted by Regional Council in August 2011, to be found at http://www.halifax.ca/city-hall/boards-committees-commissions/volunteer-boards-committees/public-appointment-policy

15.1.1 Citizen Appointments to the Regional Watersheds Advisory Board

Motion:
That Halifax and West Community Council:
1) Proceed to appoint one Citizen Representative to the Regional Watersheds Advisory Board for a term to November 2018.
2) It is further recommended that the citizen appointment be released to the public following ratification and notification of the successful candidate.
