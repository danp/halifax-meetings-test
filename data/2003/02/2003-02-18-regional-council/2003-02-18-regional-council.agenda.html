10. MOTIONS
10.1 Councillor Cooper

“To amend Administrative Order Number 1, the amendment being to add to Section 52(1) thereof an additional clause as follows:

(f) shall require for approval an affirmative vote of two-thirds of the members present.

This amendment will require that a minimum of two-thirds of the members of Council present at a meeting vote in favour of a motion that “the question be now put” in order that it be approved, thereby ensuring that an overwhelming majority of Council desires to end the debate and deal with the substantive motion on the floor before such a motion is successful.”
