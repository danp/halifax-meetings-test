Motion:
That Halifax Regional Council:
1. Transfer the $25,000 approved for a grant to the Sac-A-Wa Canoe Club, and the $25,000 approved for a grant to the Sheet Harbour and Area Chamber of Commerce under the 2017 Community Grants Program from M311-8004 to the General Contingency Reserve, Q421; and
2.  Authorize the withdrawal of $50,000 from the General Contingency Reserve to fund the two (2) capital grants, $25,000 for a grant to the Sac-A-Wa Canoe Club and the $25,000 for a grant to the Sheet Harbour and Area Chamber of Commerce, as approved under the 2017 Community Grants Program, to be incurred in 2018-19, subject to confirmation of the required Federal government funding and a demonstrated ability to proceed with construction.

14.3 EXECUTIVE STANDING COMMITTEE
14.3.1 Proposed Amendments to Administrative Order One, Respecting the Procedures of the Council - Potential Changes to the Audit and Finance Standing Committee [PDF]
Motion:
That Halifax Regional Council:
1. Adopt the amendments to Schedule 2 of Administrative Order One - the Audit and Finance Committee Terms of Reference (Attachment B to the January 26, 2018 staff report) to allow for the addition of up to two members of the public as members of the Audit Committee; and
2. Direct the Clerk’s Office, in accordance with the Public Appointment Policy, to initiate a process to recruit up to two members of the public, who have an accounting designation and experience working with or in the public sector, to join the Halifax Regional Municipality Audit Committee.
