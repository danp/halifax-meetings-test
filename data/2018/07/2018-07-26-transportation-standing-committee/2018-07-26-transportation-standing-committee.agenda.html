12.1.1 Transit Priority Corridor: Gottingen Street [PDF]

-Presentation [PDF]

Motion:
That the Transportation Standing Committee recommend that Halifax Regional Council:
1. Approve detailed design as shown in Attachment B of the staff report dated June 21, 2018.
2. Approve the parking loss mitigation plan as described in Attachment C of the staff report dated June 21, 2018.
3. Direct staff to proceed with implementation of a peak period (7am-9am and 3pm-6pm, Monday to Friday) northbound bus lane on the Gottingen Street corridor.
4. Approve the evaluation methodology as per Attachment E of this report through which the Gottingen Street peak period northbound bus lane will be measured and evaluated one year after implementation.
