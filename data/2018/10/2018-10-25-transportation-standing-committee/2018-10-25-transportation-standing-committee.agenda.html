Motion:

That the Transportation Standing Committee request a staff report on the feasibility of:

1. Implementing a Transit Pass Program similar to Kingston, Ontario’s that would provide a bus  pass to all high school students along with orientation sessions at schools;

2. Increasing the age that children ride free until from 5 to 16 as part of the fare strategy.
