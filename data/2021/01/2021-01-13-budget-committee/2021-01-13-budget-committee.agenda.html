Would you like to speak during Public Participation?

If you’d like to speak during Public Participation, contact the Municipal Clerk's Office no later than 4:30 p.m. on Tuesday, January 12 to be added to the speakers' list to speak by telephone. This meeting will occur in a virtual setting and attending in person is not possible. To register as a speaker:

  • Send an email to clerks@halifax.ca
  • Phone 902.490.4210 (Mon-Fri, 8:30 am-4:30 pm)
  • Fax 902.490.4208

In your message, state that you wish to speak during Public Participation at the January 13 Budget Committee meeting, and give your name, community of residence, email address, and the phone number that you’ll use to participate.
