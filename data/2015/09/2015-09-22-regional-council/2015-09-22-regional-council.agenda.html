12. MOTIONS

12.1 Councillor Hendsbee
That Halifax Regional Council request a staff report for the Municipality to develop policy and implement a process for the acceptance and adoption of old, abandoned or orphan cemeteries, and/or from any congregation and/or community groups that want to convey and transfer their cemeteries over to the Municipality for the purposes of ongoing maintenance and perpetual care.

12.2 Councillor Whitman
That Halifax Regional Council request a staff report that explores opportunities to provide financial/value-in-kind contributions of up to $40,000 annually to support the Province and the private sector in a successful bid to become home of the Women’s National Volleyball Team Training Centre starting in 2016/17.

12.3 Ratification of In Camera Motion from September 8, 2015
That Halifax Regional Council ratify the motion from the in camera session held on September 8, 2015 as contained in the August 21, 2015 private and confidential report.

That the private and confidential report dated August 21, 2015 not be released to the public until Union ratification votes are completed and a decision made by the HRMPP Committee regarding the July 2015 Pension Committee recommendation.
