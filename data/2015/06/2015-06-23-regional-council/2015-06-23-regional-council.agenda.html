12.2 Councillor Craig
That Halifax Regional Council direct staff to produce a report which will:
1. Clarify the current Solid Waste Management practice of collecting “bulky items”; and,
2. Provide options, including possible changes to the “Solid Waste Resource Collection and Disposal By-Law” By-Law No. S-600 for changing the “bulky items”
a. definition;
b. limits for collection;
c. frequency of collection.

12.3 Councillor Craig
That Halifax Regional Council request a staff report to:
1. Look at the social, operational, and financial implications of closing or altering programs and services that serve a distinct demographic in HRM owned facilities, as well as
2. Outline the criteria that are used in the decision making process and the policy options for any implementation and subsequent redirection/accommodation of residents when these distinct services are implemented, reduced or stopped.
