13.1.1 Case 19800: Request to permit a Construction and Demolition Waste Processing Facility at PID 40740276 Highway 7, Porters Lake [PDF]

- Staff Presentation [PDF]

Motion:
That Harbour East-Marine Drive Community Council refuse the proposed amendment to Schedule A - Zoning of the Land Use By-law for Planning Districts 8 & 9 (Lake Echo/Porters Lake), as set out in Attachment A of the staff report dated December 12, 2019, which rezones a 5.9 hectare (14.7 acre) portion of a property located along the north side of Highway 7 and west of Parker Lane in Porters Lake (PID 40740276) from RE (Rural Enterprise) to CD-2 (Construction and Demolition Materials Processing Facilities) to allow a Construction and Demolition (C&D) Waste Processing Facility.

13.1.2 Case 21537: Development Agreement for 169 Wyse Road, Dartmouth [PDF]
Motion:
That Harbour East-Marine Drive Community Council give notice of motion to consider the proposed development agreement, as set out in Attachment A of the staff report dated January 4, 2019, to allow for a 6-storey residential building containing ground floor commercial uses at 169 Wyse Road, Dartmouth and schedule a public hearing.

13.1.3 Case 22059: Dartmouth Land Use By-law Amendment (Rezoning) for 24 Hester Street, Dartmouth [PDF]
Motion:
That Harbour East-Marine Drive Community Council give First Reading to consider approval of the proposed amendment to the Land Use By-law for Dartmouth, as set out in Attachment A, to rezone 24 Hester Street, Dartmouth from the R-1 (Single Family Dwelling) Zone to the R-2 (Two Family Residential) Zone and schedule a public hearing.

13.1.4 Case 21552: Rezoning for Lancaster Drive and Woodland Avenue, Dartmouth [PDF]
Motion:
That Harbour East-Marine Drive Community Council give First Reading to consider approval of the proposed amendment to the Land Use By-Law for Dartmouth, as set out in Attachment A, to rezone lands at the corner of Lancaster Drive and Woodland Avenue from the R-1 (Single Family Residential) Zone to R-3 (Multiple Family Residential) Zone – Medium Density, and schedule a public hearing.
