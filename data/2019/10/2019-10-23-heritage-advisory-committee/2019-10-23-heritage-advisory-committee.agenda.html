9.1 Case H00468 Supplemental Report for 26 Elliot Street, Dartmouth
(i)Supplementary report dated September 19, 2019 [PDF]
(ii)Heritage Advisory Committee report dated July 25, 2019 [PDF]

-Staff Presentation [PDF]
Motion:
That the Heritage Advisory Committee recommends that Halifax Regional Council proceed with the approval of the request to include 26 Elliot Street, Dartmouth in the Registry of Heritage Property for the Halifax Regional Municipality as a municipal heritage property without the inclusion of interior character defining elements.

9.2 Case H00446: Old South Suburb Heritage Conservation District Plan, Bylaw, and Amendments to the Downtown Halifax Secondary Municipal Planning Strategy and Land Use Bylaw [PDF]

-Staff Presentation [PDF]
Motion:
That the Heritage Advisory Committee recommends that Halifax Regional Council:
1. Give First Reading to consider the Old South Suburb Heritage Conservation District Plan and the Old South Suburb Heritage Conservation District By-law as contained in Attachments A and B, of the staff report dated September 25, 2019 and schedule a public hearing;
2. Give First Reading to consider the amendments to the Downtown Halifax Municipal Planning Strategy and the amendments to the Downtown Halifax Land Use By-law as contained in Attachments C and D of the staff report dated September 25, 2019 and schedule a public hearing; and
3. Adopt the Old South Suburb Heritage Conservation District Plan, the Old South Suburb Heritage Conservation District By-law and amendments to the Downtown Halifax Municipal Planning Strategy for Halifax and amendments to the Downtown Halifax Land Use By-law , as set out in Attachments A, B, C, and D of the staff report dated September 25, 2019.

10. MOTIONS/DISCUSSION
10.1 William Breckenridge – 5925 Spring Garden Road [PDF]
Motion:
That the Heritage Advisory Committee recommend that Halifax Regional Council request a staff report examining the potential for including 5925 Spring Garden Road, 1538 Carlton Street and 1540 Carlton Street by Camp Hill Cemetery in the Registry of Heritage Property for the Halifax Regional Municipality.
