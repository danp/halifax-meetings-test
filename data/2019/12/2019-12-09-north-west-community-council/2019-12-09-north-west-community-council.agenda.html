7.1 Rescission of North West Community Council Motion 13.2.1 - November 4, 2019 Timberlane Terrace Park and Grant Line Trail  [PDF]

Motion:
That North West Community Council:
1. Waive section 63 (3) of Administrative Order One respecting notice of motion for a motion of Rescission;
2. Rescind the November 4, 2019 recommending that Halifax Regional Council authorize a one-time contribution of $25,000 from the Hammonds Plains Area Rate surplus to the Hammonds Plains Rate Payers Association to complete the development of Timberlane Terrace Park and capital improvements to the Grant Line Trail;
3. Recommend that Halifax Regional Council:
•    Authorize a one-time contribution of $25,000 from the Hammonds Plains Area Rate surplus to the Highland Park Rate Payers Association to complete the development of Timberlane Terrace Park; and
•    Authorize an additional one-time contribution of $25,000 from the Hammond Plains Area Rate surplus to the Highland Park Rate Payers Association for capital improvements to the Grant Line Trail
