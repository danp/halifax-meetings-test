9.1.1 Case 22334: Application by WM Fares requesting to enter into a development agreement to allow for a one storey commercial building at 1401 Sackville Drive, Middle Sackville

The applicant is proposing a one-story, 7,000 square foot commercial building with 41 parking spaces in the rear of the property.

The existing zoning does not permit stand alone commercial uses on the property, however, Policy UR-20 of the Sackville Municipal Planning Strategy does enable the request to enter into a Development Agreement to permit commercial service uses on the property.

In preparing the recommendation to North West Community Council, the Committee is being asked to assess potential impact on the community. The following is before the Committee:
• Compatibility with adjacent land uses;
• Quality of landscaping and buffering from adjacent residential properties and travelling public; and
• Traffic impacts.

The following is before the Committee:
- Project Rationale
- Building Plans
- Traffic Impact Statement

These documents and other relevant information are available on the case website:
https://www.halifax.ca/business/planning-development/applications/case-22334-1401-sackville-dr-middle-sackville

Information at the above link will be updated as required. For a copy of the materials presented to the Planning Advisory Committee, please contact the Clerk’s Office.
